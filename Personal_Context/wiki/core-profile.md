# Core Profile

Samson Schirmer is a Senior Product Manager at Comcast in the Marketing Creative Technology group, leading the GenAI Center of Excellence. His work sits at the intersection of AI tooling, creative workflow transformation, product strategy, and enterprise change management.

His current professional focus is moving Comcast marketing from manual, agency-dependent workflows toward AI-native, agentic systems. The major active workstreams are Writer agent deployment, Adobe GenStudio rollout, platform integration across Workfront/Figma/Adobe, Chennai team onboarding, and the broader evolution toward an in-house AI Production Studio.

The recurring philosophy is simple: AI should scale human creativity, not replace it. The hard part is adoption, not the technology. Samson prefers demos over memos, validated numbers over projections, and systems that work without him explaining them every time.

Outside work, Samson runs Svenson Labs as a personal agent lab for experiments in prediction markets, sports underdogs, children's storytelling, local AI, and other side projects. He also built Safe Home, a privacy-first home AI concept using Ollama, Open Web UI, Docker, and Raspberry Pi, which later became a potential Comcast product exploration.
