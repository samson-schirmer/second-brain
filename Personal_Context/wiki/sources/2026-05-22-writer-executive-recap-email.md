# Source Note: Writer Executive Recap Email Revision

## What This Source Captures

Samson revised an executive recap email for a Writer AI follow-up. The revision is a useful voice sample for executive communication: concise, direct, lightly structured, and action-oriented.

## Communication Style Takeaways

- Opens with thanks, then immediately states the purpose.
- Uses "Key themes" rather than a heavier executive-summary frame.
- Keeps bullets short and plain.
- Names governance, tool positioning, cost, renewal, and usage visibility directly.
- References the deck as a practical link, not as a formal attachment narrative.
- Uses "I've added" and "We'll follow up" to keep ownership and next steps clear.
- Avoids over-polished consultant phrasing.
- The tone is professional but still sounds like a working operator.
