# Demos Over Memos

Samson's core operating mantra. Proof through demonstration beats slides and strategy decks.

**What it means in practice:**
- Build a working prototype before pitching. Safe Home was built on a Raspberry Pi at home before it ever got organizational attention.
- De-risk through small tests rather than perfect rollout plans. Run one agent with one team and see what actually happens.
- Favor execution over planning — bias toward action, validated by getting hands-on.

**Where it shows up:**
- [[safe-home]] — proved concept at home, then pitched with working demo
- [[writer-agent-deployment]] — roll out one agent, show results, expand
- Leadership interactions — especially with [[ryan-craig]] (show up prepared, be bold) and [[grove]] (show up with structure, not half-baked ideas)

**Relationship to other principles:** Part of a broader orientation toward pragmatism over perfection, adoption over feature completeness, execution over strategy decks.
