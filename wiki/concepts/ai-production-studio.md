# AI Production Studio

Samson's longer-term vision (1-3 years) for where the GenAI COE evolves.

**From:** AI enablement function — helping other teams adopt AI tools.

**To:** In-house AI Production Studio — recruiting the best creative builders and owning content creation directly, starting with social content (Instagram, TikTok).

**The moat:** Human creative judgment and cultural fluency. GenAI efficiency + human creative confidence = premium, high-value content team. Culture-led, not tech-led.

**Why this matters:** The team becomes irreplaceable by AI because of creative taste and brand awareness — the exact thing AI can't replicate. The technology makes them efficient; the taste makes them indispensable.

**Prerequisite:** Current [[marketing-workflow-transformation]] succeeds first. Can't own production until enablement is proven.
