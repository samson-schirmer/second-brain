# Push to Pull

A maturity signal Samson uses to measure whether the GenAI COE is succeeding.

**Push (current state):** Samson is still opening doors, explaining what the COE does, convincing teams to try AI tools. Every new team requires effort to engage.

**Pull (target state):** Teams come to the COE because they've heard what Writer agents did for another team and they want in. The value proposition travels on its own. The business transformation story is packaged, measured, and being told at the SVP level without Samson pushing it there.

**Why this is the success signal:** "Systems that work without me explaining them every time." When the COE flips from push to pull, Samson knows the change management has taken root — adoption is self-sustaining.

**Six-month goal:** Achieve this flip for Writer agent deployment specifically.
