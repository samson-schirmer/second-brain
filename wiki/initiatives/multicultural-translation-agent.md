# Multicultural Translation Agent

**What:** A Writer-based agent/workflow for Spanish translation and multicultural content support.

**Status:** Active and gaining traction.

**Current operating need:** Samson wants a recurring weekly or biweekly feedback session with a small group of the right Spanish-language stakeholders. The goal is to demo, collect feedback, and make stakeholders feel they have a voice in how the translation agent is trained.

**Stakeholder nuance:** A later clarification in the transcript says one Spanish-experience team is not the team that writes or owns translations. They review whether Spanish experiences are working, identify misses, and can help share that the tool exists with other teams. Brand-owned terminology and messaging decisions should stay with the brand owner before being added to Brand Hub and reflected in Writer.

**Demo guidance:** For that team, keep the demo high-level: what the tool is, what it can do, and how it may be shared. Do not turn the session into detailed terminology governance unless the brand owner asks for that.

**Related opportunities:** A Google image / translator workflow was discussed as a possible v1 hybrid, but Samson marked the QR-code idea as lower priority because it is not what stakeholders are currently asking for.
