# Marketing Workflow Transformation

**What:** Moving Comcast marketing from manual, agency-dependent workflows to AI-native, agentic ones. This is the overarching initiative that encompasses [[writer-agent-deployment]], [[adobe-genstudio-rollout]], and [[platform-integration]].

**Status:** In progress (overarching).

**Owner:** Samson Schirmer / GenAI COE.

**Longer-term evolution:** Transition from AI enablement to an in-house [[ai-production-studio]] that owns content creation directly, starting with social (Instagram, TikTok).

**Success signal:** Systems that work without Samson explaining them every time. Pull instead of push. Team operating independently.
