# Platform Integration

**What:** Connecting AI tooling into existing marketing platforms — Workfront, Figma, Adobe, Writer, and Microsoft 365.

**Status:** In progress.

**Owner:** Samson Schirmer.

**Philosophy:** Integrate into existing tools over building standalone AI destinations. Meet people where they work — the bottleneck is always adoption, not technology.

**Key platforms:** Workfront (project management), Figma (design), Adobe Creative Suite (production), Microsoft Teams (collaboration), Writer connectors into Outlook/Teams/SharePoint/OneDrive/Calendar.

## Current integration questions

- Adobe/Writer: Samson wants to understand whether Adobe has appetite for an agent plug-in like Writer across its platforms, or whether the team should connect Writer to individual Adobe products directly through APIs.
- Adobe stack: The team is exploring Workfront, Frame, Creative Cloud, and unified storage implications.
- Microsoft stack: Writer connector access depends on the MCP server request through My Comcast Access.
