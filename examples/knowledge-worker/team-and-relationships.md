# Team and Relationships

## Priya Patel

**Role:** VP of Product
**Relationship:** My manager.
**How We Interact:** Weekly 1:1 (Thursdays, 30 min), plus async in Slack throughout the week.
**What She Needs From Me:** Clear status on my product area, early warning on risks, well-structured proposals for anything that needs her sign-off. She hates surprises.
**What I Need From Her:** Air cover with leadership when priorities conflict, quick decisions on escalations, and honest feedback on my specs before I take them to eng.
**Context for Agents:** Priya is direct and moves fast. She reads docs before meetings — never walk in expecting to present something she hasn't seen. She prefers bullet points over paragraphs. If you're drafting something for her, keep it under one page.

## James Whitfield

**Role:** Engineering Lead, Team Coral
**Relationship:** My closest engineering partner.
**How We Interact:** Daily standup, Monday 1:1, constant Slack. We sit near each other and talk informally a lot.
**What He Needs From Me:** Clear, unambiguous specs. Stable priorities that don't shift mid-sprint. Context on why we're building something, not just what.
**What I Need From Him:** Honest estimates, early flags when something is harder than expected, and pushback when my specs have technical blind spots.
**Context for Agents:** James is thoughtful and thorough. He won't rush an answer. If you're prepping me for a meeting with him, focus on technical specifics — he'll want to go deep on implementation details.

## Maya Rodriguez

**Role:** Product Designer
**Relationship:** Embedded designer for my product area.
**How We Interact:** Design reviews weekly, async in Figma comments and Slack.
**What She Needs From Me:** Clear problem framing, user research context, and freedom to explore before I lock down requirements.
**What I Need From Her:** Design work that's grounded in real user behavior, not just aesthetically clean.
**Context for Agents:** Maya thinks visually. Share screenshots and mockups, not lengthy written descriptions.

## Dr. Adaeze Okafor

**Role:** Chief Medical Officer (part-time clinical advisor to product)
**Relationship:** Clinical subject matter expert for my product area.
**How We Interact:** Biweekly check-in, ad hoc when I need clinical validation on a feature.
**What She Needs From Me:** Respect for her time — she's a practicing physician. Come prepared, ask specific questions, don't ramble.
**What I Need From Her:** Clinical judgment calls on feature design, validation that our workflows match real clinical practice.
**Context for Agents:** Extremely busy. If prepping an agenda for her, put the most important question first. She'll answer what she can in the time she has.
